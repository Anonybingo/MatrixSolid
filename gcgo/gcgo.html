Problem Statement

The problem, plainly stated...

The difficulty occurs when a patient needs to know about their medicines or if it is possible to find a pharmacy with a medicine. In most instances they need to call several times or go to the pharmacy to obtain information. This may be time consuming and treatment can be delayed. In the coming 12 months, we will be creating a site where patients will be able to directly access pharmacists. Patients will have the opportunity to ask a question about their current medicine(s), to check if a medicine is available, to request that medicine to be re-filled, and to take advice from a pharmacist without having to visit the pharmacy. The solution will improve patient-pharmacist interaction, further optimize use of time and facilitate patient access to medications.
