Grand Challenge

OUR GCGO

Every E-Lab team anchors its work to a Grand Challenge Goal. Here's the one we chose, why it fit, and how it runs through all six challenges.

The Goal

Healthcare

You cannot build a thriving economy or educate children if the population is plagued by preventable illnesses. Investing in primary healthcare is needed to improving youth employment, educational attainment and economic growth. For the Think Tank, improving healthcare is needed to solving the other 6 Grand Challenges.

Problem Statement

The problem, plainly stated...

The difficulty occurs when a patient needs to know about their medicines or if it is possible to find a pharmacy with a medicine. In most instances they need to call several times or go to the pharmacy to obtain information. This may be time consuming and treatment can be delayed. In the coming 12 months, we will be creating a site where patients will be able to directly access pharmacists. Patients will have the opportunity to ask a question about their current medicine(s) , to check if a medicine is available and to take advice from a pharmacist without having to visit the pharmacy. The solution will improve patient and pharmacist interaction, further optimize use of time and facilitate patient access to medications.

Our Solution

What we're proposing

We will be creating a website that will allow customers to get in contact with pharmacies and have their medication delivered. It will work by allowing users to send prescriptions to pharmacists. The pharmacists confirm that it is from a reliable source and the drugs availability. The pharmacist then hands the medicine over to a moto who has an app with directions to the patients location which they already entered.It will largley benefit patients who don't know which pharmacies have thier medication in stock. People with mobility issues or with time sensitive needs for medication. As well as people who would want to save transport money to and from a pharmacy. What makes us unique is that we would be the first web based medicine delivery service in Rwanda.
