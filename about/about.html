Who We Are

THE TEAM BEHIND THE MATRIX

Matrix Solid formed inside African Leadership University's E-Lab, where every term hands students a real challenge instead of a hypothetical one. We chose to treat six separate assignments as one continuous body of work, run by the same small team, the same way, every time.

How We Work

Same method, every challenge.

Define

We start by defining the problem properly before touching a solution — most bad answers come from solving the wrong question.

Evidence

Every recommendation is backed by research, not assumption — data, interviews, and fieldwork wherever we can get it.

Build

We ship something real: a prototype, a plan, a pitch — not just a slide deck of ideas.

Present

Each challenge closes with a film, because good thinking that isn't communicated well doesn't move anyone.
